# How to Add Your Photo

Your portfolio website is ready, but you need to add your photo file!

## Quick Steps:

1. **Find your photo** on your computer
2. **Copy it** to this folder: `/Users/sashank/Personal portfolio/`
3. **Rename each photo correctly:**
   - Profile photo → `photo.jpg`
   - Football achievement photo → `trophy.jpg`
   - (You can also use `.jpeg` or `.png` extensions if needed)

## Supported Formats:
- JPG/JPEG
- PNG
- WebP

## After Adding:
1. Refresh your browser (press F5 or Cmd+R)
2. Both photos will appear automatically!
